Welcome, Dungeon Master

This toolkit will assist you in whatever magical adventures you imagine.

Choose 'Roll Dice' to generate a random dice roll. No more chasing dice across the dining room floor! Choose any number of dice and get to the results quicker, leaving more time to campaign.

Are your adventurers taking on goblins that are too weak, or dragons that are too strong? Use the 'Encounter Calculator', it will help you determine the ideal strength of foe based on the party's level.
